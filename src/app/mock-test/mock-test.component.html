GRE Mock Test

Use the GREPrep Mock Test to help you prepare for the computer-delivered GRE General Test. The mock tests are designed to help you:
  • Understand what's being tested Gain familiarity with the various question types
  • Practice your test-taking strategies, such as time management, by taking a test under timed conditions
  • Use the concepts you have prepared in quantitative and verbal question types

Instructions
  • The Mock Test will be held for 2 hours i.e. 120 mins which will have 50 questions.
  • The questions consist of Verbal and Quantitative types.
  • The questions can either be multiple choice or single choice types
  • Once you submit your test, you can get your mock test score
  • You can use buttons like First, Previous, Next, and Last to move to questions
  • The Mock Test does not include the Analytical Writing
  • Click on Start Your Mock Test button below to start your Mock Test.
  • Once you click on the button, the test starts immediately and the clock starts automatically
  • All the best for your Mock Test!!
